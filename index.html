The Ultimate Clubhouse Experience

At Golf Cove, we redefine indoor entertainment by blending state-of-the-art technology with a welcoming and vibrant atmosphere. Here's why The Cove stands out as your ultimate clubhouse experience for fun, relaxation, and memorable events.

1

Cutting-Edge Virtual Golf & MultiSport Arcade

Experience the thrill of realistic and engaging virtual sports. Our advanced simulators offer a wide variety of games, including golf, soccer, hockey, and more. Whether you're perfecting your swing or enjoying a friendly match with friends, our virtual arcade ensures endless fun for all ages and skill levels.

2

Versatile Event Spaces

Host your next event at Golf Cove and leave a lasting impression. Our private rooms and flexible event packages are perfect for birthdays, corporate gatherings, and family celebrations. Whether you're looking to plan an intimate party or a large group event, we provide the ideal setting to create unforgettable memories.

3

Exclusive Membership Perks

Join the Golf Cove community and unlock exclusive benefits. Our individual and corporate memberships offer priority booking, discounted rates, and special access to member-only events. Enhance your social and professional life with our unique blend of entertainment and networking opportunities.

4

Delicious Catering and Full-Service Bar

Enjoy a wide selection of craft beers, cocktails, and delicious food from our full-service kitchen. Our catering options are perfect for any event, providing a delightful dining experience to complement your activities.

5

Unmatched Customer Experience

Your satisfaction is our top priority. Our friendly and professional staff are dedicated to providing exceptional service, ensuring your visit is enjoyable and hassle-free. From the moment you arrive, you'll feel welcomed and valued.

6

Convenient Location

Located in the heart of North Haven, Connecticut, Golf Cove is easily accessible and provides ample parking. Whether you're local or visiting from out of town, our facility is designed to be a convenient and enjoyable destination for all.

Choose The Cove for your next outing, event, or family day out, and discover why we're the top choice for indoor entertainment and events in North Haven. Experience the best in virtual sports, exceptional service, and a fun-filled atmosphere that you'll want to return to again and again.
